The inspiration comes from three classic pixel games. The modified version allows you to have a battle with your friends!

There are 3 levels, 

1 Level based on Tennies for Two(1958). The player on the left presses the space key to hit the ball, and the player on the right presses the enter key to hit the ball.

2 Level based on Pac-Man(1980). The player on the left moves using the WASD keys, the player on the right moves using the direction keys.

3 Level based on Flappy Bird(2013). The player on the left presses the space key to fly, the player on the right presses the enter key to set a pair of pipes.
